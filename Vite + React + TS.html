Protect yourself from financial fraud

Fraud Prevention Tips

  • Never share your bank account details or passwords with anyone.
  • Use strong, unique passwords for all your financial accounts.
  • Be cautious of unsolicited emails or phone calls asking for your financial information.
  • Enable two-factor authentication on all your financial accounts when possible.
